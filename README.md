## üéÆ Features

### üèÜ Progression Systems
- **Ascendancy System** - Soul XP and prestige upgrades with 9 unique upgrade paths
- **Skills System** - Mining, Woodcutting, Combat, Defense, Farming, Smithing
- **Economy System** - Earn money through gameplay, spend at the comprehensive shop

### üõ†Ô∏è Quality of Life
- **Vein Mining** - Hold SHIFT while breaking ores to break entire veins
- **Lumberjack** - Hold SHIFT while breaking logs to fell entire trees
- **Void Magnet** - Toggle with [N] - pulls items and voids junk for currency
- **Auto Replant** - Crops automatically replant when harvested at maturity
- **Sleep Sooner** - Double-right-click any bed to sleep regardless of time
- **Death Safety** - Keep your inventory on death, with XP preservation
- **Infinite Trading** - Villager trades never lock out from overuse
- **Trade Cycling** - Break lecterns to easily cycle librarian trades

### ‚ú® Utility Features
- **Full Brightness Toggle** - Press [B] for instant night vision toggle
- **GUI Clock** - In-game time displayed in your HUD
- **Starter Kit** - New players receive tools, armor, food, and utility items
- **Pet Names** - Tamed animals display owner-based names automatically
- **Hoe Tweaks** - Harvest+replant with right-click, SHIFT+right-click for 3x3 tilling
- **Silkier Touch** - Silk touch now works on spawners and normally unobtainable blocks
- **Despawning Eggs Hatch** - Eggs about to despawn hatch into chickens instead

### üìä HUD & Feedback
- **Sidebar HUD** - Persistent display showing Soul XP, Balance, Vanilla XP, Health, Food, Coordinates
- **Action Bar Feedback** - Floating messages for money/XP gains (+50 Gold, +10 Soul XP)
- **Clock Display** - Time of day shown in HUD

### üõí Expanded Shop
The shop now features **10 categories** with **100+ items**:
- **Tools** - Pickaxes, axes, shovels (all tiers)
- **Armor** - Full armor sets from leather to netherite
- **Weapons** - Swords, bows, crossbows, tridents
- **Food** - Basic to gourmet food items
- **Blocks** - Building materials and decorative blocks
- **Ores & Gems** - Raw ores, ingots, gems
- **Spawn Eggs** - Various mob spawn eggs
- **Utility** - Ender pearls, totems, rockets, maps
- **Enchanted** - Pre-enchanted premium gear
- **Rare** - Dragon eggs, hearts of the sea, conduits

## ‚å®Ô∏è Keybinds

### Row 1 - GUI Utilities
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **G** | Open Shop |
| **H** | Toggle HUD Sidebar |
| **J** | Open Ascendancy Screen |
| **K** | Open Skills Screen |

### Row 2 - Feature Toggles
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **B** | Toggle Full Brightness |
| **N** | Toggle Void Magnet |
| **M** | Toggle Squat Grow |

### Shift Mechanics
| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **SHIFT + Break Ore** | Vein Mining (breaks entire vein) |
| **SHIFT + Break Log** | Lumberjack (fells entire tree) |
| **SHIFT + Right-click with Hoe** | Till 3x3 area |

### Bed Mechanics
| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Right-click bed** | Normal bed behavior |
| **Double-right-click bed** | Sleep at any time of day |

4. Launch Minecraft 1.21.11
